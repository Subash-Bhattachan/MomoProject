Tools Needed
1. Dumpling Wrapper from Korean Food Market (100 for 5 people)
2. A bowl of water from kitchen tap
3. A fork to scoop and lift the filling
4. Desired filling of your choice, for sure!
What else you need?
1. Tons of patience!
2. Readiness to get hands wet and dirty
3. Helping hands. The more the better for bigger serving groups!
4. Zeal to showcase your skills learnt!
